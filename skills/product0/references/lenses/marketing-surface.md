# Marketing-surface lens

Use this lens for landing pages, conversion pages, marketing sites, and other
surfaces whose primary job is to shape a buyer's understanding and next action.
Read the product, current pages, sales material, customer language, analytics,
and credible proof before recommending a direction. Direction approval is prohibited
until every relevant marker below is resolved or explicitly marked as a non-blocking
assumption.

## MS01_TARGET_BUYER_AND_BUYING_SITUATION

- **Repository evidence to inspect:** audience research, CRM or support language, pricing, and the acquisition path.
- **Product0 recommendation:** name the priority buyer, their urgency, and the moment that makes this page useful.
- **Blocking product decision:** resolve a contested primary audience or buying situation.
- **Reserved for technical brainstorming:** tracking implementation and audience-segmentation mechanics.

## MS02_PAGE_JOB_AND_DESIRED_PERCEPTION

- **Repository evidence to inspect:** entry source, existing funnel steps, competitors, and page performance.
- **Product0 recommendation:** state the one page job and the perception visitors should leave with.
- **Blocking product decision:** choose when the page must serve incompatible jobs or perceptions.
- **Reserved for technical brainstorming:** routing, rendering, and responsive layout techniques.

## MS03_POSITIONING_AND_VALUE_PROPOSITION

- **Repository evidence to inspect:** product capabilities, differentiated outcomes, alternatives, and customer proof.
- **Product0 recommendation:** recommend a defensible value proposition and contrast with the relevant alternative.
- **Blocking product decision:** settle a claim that changes product positioning or promises an unproven outcome.
- **Reserved for technical brainstorming:** content models and component composition.

## MS04_MESSAGE_HIERARCHY

- **Repository evidence to inspect:** current copy, buyer objections, search intent, and sales-call themes.
- **Product0 recommendation:** order the primary promise, supporting reasons, proof, and calls to action.
- **Blocking product decision:** decide among materially different lead messages for distinct strategic audiences.
- **Reserved for technical brainstorming:** typography tokens, truncation, and localization plumbing.

## MS05_PAGE_NARRATIVE

- **Repository evidence to inspect:** available proof, product journey, conversion data, and related pages.
- **Product0 recommendation:** propose a section order that moves from problem and promise to proof and action.
- **Blocking product decision:** resolve whether a narrative must educate, compare, qualify, or convert first.
- **Reserved for technical brainstorming:** section components, scroll effects, and generic responsive behavior.

## MS06_PRODUCT_DEMONSTRATION_AND_VISUAL_DIRECTION

- **Repository evidence to inspect:** product UI, brand assets, demo recordings, and claims each visual can substantiate.
- **Product0 recommendation:** specify the product moments and visual treatment that make the promise believable.
- **Blocking product decision:** decide when a visual implies a capability, persona, or result the product does not support.
- **Reserved for technical brainstorming:** image formats, asset optimization, and interaction implementation.

## MS07_PROOF_TRUST_AND_CREDIBILITY

- **Repository evidence to inspect:** customer evidence, security posture, testimonials, case studies, and approval constraints.
- **Product0 recommendation:** prioritize the strongest truthful proof and identify credibility gaps rather than inventing proof.
- **Blocking product decision:** obtain authority for sensitive logos, testimonials, comparative claims, or certifications.
- **Reserved for technical brainstorming:** logo grids, embeds, and routine accessibility implementation.

## MS08_BUYER_OBJECTIONS

- **Repository evidence to inspect:** sales objections, churn reasons, support tickets, and competitor comparisons.
- **Product0 recommendation:** answer the objections that would otherwise stop the intended buyer from progressing.
- **Blocking product decision:** resolve a genuine product limitation, policy, or promise exposed by an objection.
- **Reserved for technical brainstorming:** accordions, modal behavior, keyboard interaction, and validation defaults.

## MS09_CONVERSION_STRATEGY

- **Repository evidence to inspect:** funnel metrics, sales motion, qualification needs, and the available next steps.
- **Product0 recommendation:** set a primary CTA, supporting CTA, qualification threshold, and conversion hierarchy.
- **Blocking product decision:** choose between materially different conversion motions such as self-serve and sales-led.
- **Reserved for technical brainstorming:** form fields, submission handling, loading states, and generic error presentation.

## MS10_MEASUREMENT_AND_CLAIM_SAFETY

- **Repository evidence to inspect:** analytics definitions, legal guidance, product telemetry, and substantiation for claims.
- **Product0 recommendation:** name decision-useful measures and guardrails for accurate, qualified marketing claims.
- **Blocking product decision:** approve a claim, measurement target, or data use with legal, trust, or strategic consequences.
- **Reserved for technical brainstorming:** analytics SDK selection, event schemas, and cookie-banner mechanics.
