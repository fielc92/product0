# Product0 readiness rubric

Use this rubric after a complete brief is in handoff-draft. The deterministic
brief linter supports structural authoring QA; it never replaces this semantic
product review. Return only HANDOFF_READY or NOT_READY.

## Evidence and provenance

- SL01_REPOSITORY_EVIDENCE_MISSING: available repository context is absent
  from the direction. NOT_READY until Product0 inspects and cites it.
- SL02_ASSUMPTION_PROMOTED_TO_FACT: a recommendation or inference is stated
  as confirmed. NOT_READY until provenance is restored.
- SL08_BUSINESS_CONTEXT_STILL_MISSING: the buyer, business outcome, or
  commercial context needed to judge the decision is unresolved. NOT_READY.

## Useful direction

- SL03_DECISION_REPEATED_ACROSS_SECTIONS: the same decision is repeated
  without adding meaning. NOT_READY until the brief is concise.
- SL04_ONE_ITEM_PRODUCT_SLICE: a slice has no independent outcome or release
  boundary. NOT_READY until it is removed or made meaningful.
- SL05_IRRELEVANT_TECHNICAL_QUESTION: a product brief delegates an avoidable
  technical choice. NOT_READY until it returns to technical brainstorming.
- SL06_ROUTINE_UX_DOMINATES_DIRECTION: routine states crowd out strategic
  product direction. NOT_READY until strategy is restored.
- SL07_DISPROPORTIONATE_LENGTH: length exceeds its decision value. NOT_READY
  until rationale or concision makes it proportionate.

## Marketing-surface readiness

- MSR01_MESSAGE_HIERARCHY_MISSING: no clear priority of messages. NOT_READY.
- MSR02_PAGE_NARRATIVE_MISSING: no coherent narrative sequence. NOT_READY.
- MSR03_VISUAL_DIRECTION_MISSING: product demonstration or visual direction
  is missing. NOT_READY.
- MSR04_PROOF_OR_TRUST_MISSING: proof, trust, or claim safety is absent.
  NOT_READY.
- MSR05_OBJECTION_HANDLING_MISSING: consequential buyer objections are not
  addressed. NOT_READY.
- MSR06_CONVERSION_STRATEGY_MISSING: next action and conversion intent are
  unspecified. NOT_READY.

Editorial defects may be fixed in the review. Strategic gaps return to
direction shaping; do not silently invent direction after approval.
